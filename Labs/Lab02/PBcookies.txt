peanut butter cookies

ingredients
1 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2.5 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1.5 teaspoon baking soda
0.5 teaspoon salt
additional white sugar for coating

Method of Prep
cream butter and sugars. mix eggs in one at a time. in separate bowl, sift together dry ingredients. slowly stir into butter mixture. place dough in refrigerator 1 hour. preheat oven to 375 degrees F. use small scoop to make consistent dough balls, rolling in sugar before placing on baking sheet. flatten each ball with fork, making criss-cross pattern. bake 8-10 minutes. do not overbake. cool on wire rack.
